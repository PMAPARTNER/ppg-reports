Direct Channel Marketing Intent

PMAPARTNER

Securing Direct Booking Value

The summer window between **June 15 and October 15** represents Cavo Marina's highest yield period in Corfu. Historically, third-party OTAs intercept high-intent customers at checkout, charging up to 25% in commission fees.

This campaign architecture is designed to capture travelers when they perform brand searches or look for beachfront suites. By routing them to the direct website, we secure bookings directly in our PMS database, bypass OTA fees, and build long-term guest loyalty.

Our combined marketing budget of **€8,000** for this high-season window (strictly capped at **€2,000 per month**) targets premium beach-seeking demographics in the UK, Germany, Italy, and Poland, driving an estimated **€72,500** in direct revenue at a target blended ROAS of **9.06x**.

Strategic Recommendations

PMAPARTNER
01

Enforce Direct Rate Parity

Ensure the direct website rate is always equal to or slightly lower than rates displayed on OTAs. If Booking.com undercuts the direct rate through Genius, utilize dynamic pricing controls to remain competitive.

02

Establish Google Hotel Ads Price Feed

Directly connect the PMS/Direct Booking rate feed to Google Maps. When travelers search for Cavomarina, they will see direct rates alongside OTA rates.

03

Implement Dynamic Abandonment Retargeting

Leverage Meta and Google pixel tracking to identify users who started the reservation process but did not complete it, offering direct-booking perks.
