Uygulama Yaklaşımı

Uygulanan huni yaklaşımı: PMAX + Türkiye Arama + Avrupa + Rusya bölümleri Belconti'yi henüz tanımayan "yeni misafir" kitlesini hedefler. Russia / Yandex

Strategic Assessment — First-Mover Advantage

Belconti has not previously conducted direct advertising activity in the Russian market; Meta's unavailability in that market has precluded access to this demand. Yandex Ads provides this access for the first time, coinciding with Aeroflot's expanded flight capacity. The fact that most competing hotels are not yet present on Yandex represents an advantage for early market entry.

Given that this is a new and untested channel, ₺70,000 has been allocated as a controlled pilot budget. Scaling in August will be recommended based on performance data from the first 2-3 weeks. No booking target is provided at this stage, as no historical performance data yet exists for this channel.
