🎯 The plan in 4 simple points

  1. We're not increasing budget, we're reallocating it smartly. Real spend over the last 30 days is already ~₺598,500 — so ₺700,000 (±₺50,000, excl. VAT) is basically "what we're already spending." The only change: shifting money from weak campaigns to strong ones.

  3. We're adding free growth alongside paid. A separate SEO plan is included — it doesn't consume the ad budget at all, it grows organic traffic.
  4. Everything is explained simply, with numbers and visuals. 6 simple sections, one currency (TRY), charts throughout. Use the top-right toggles for Dark/Light and TR/EN, and the Approve button once you're ready.
